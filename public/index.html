Awareness Tips

How to avoid getting hooked

  • Check the sender domain closely for misspellings or lookalikes.
  • Never trust urgency alone. Verify through a separate official channel.
  • Hover over links before clicking and inspect the destination.
  • Be cautious with attachments requesting macros, passwords, or payment.
